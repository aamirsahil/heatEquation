The system on mathematical grid

  • The entire system is put in a 1D coordinate system(\(z\)), and vectors are introduced to represent forces.
  • Forces acting in this case are,(click on any pressure element in the picture),
    • Force from pressure element on top,$$ F_1 = P_{i+1} \times A $$
    • Weight of the pressure element,$$ F_2 = \rho Agdz $$
    • Force from pressure element below,$$ F_3 = P_{i} \times A $$
  • Here \(A\) is the cross-section area of the pressure element and \(dz\) it's thickness
