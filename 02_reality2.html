Idealization

  • Strip away unnecessary details to focus on problem at hand.
  • Real life systems are complex systems, with lots factors at play simultaneosly, in such cases we should extract the essential details by making rational approximatioms.
  • Our objective is then to discard all parameters one by one and reach a minimal model that best represent the system.
