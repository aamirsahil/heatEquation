Descretization

Descretization basically means to divide the space into smaller elements such that physics is same in all the individual elements

  • For starter we divide the space to descrete elements.
  • Each element have a constant P,V,T values and is thus a equilibrium system.
  • The boundary can exchange heat and mass.
  • But since each element is equilibrium, the net transfer is 0.
  • The model becomes closer to reality as the resolution of descretization sharpens
