The system is on a mathematical grid !

  • We place the discretised rod on the \(x\) axis and zoom in on the graph.
  • Position and temperature of its neighbouring points at time \(t\) are denoted by \(T (x + dx, t)\) and \(T (x - dx, t)\)
  • The variation of temperature \(T\) of a mass point at \(x\) with time \(t\) is proportional to the difference in temperature with its neighbouring points,$$ T(x + dx, t) - T(x, t) \text{ and } T(x - dx, t) - T(x, t) $$
Drag the slider on the right to see variation in Temperature, \(T\), with position, \(x\)!
\( (\frac{\partial T}{\partial t})_{t=t_{i}} \propto [T(x_{i} + dx, t_{i}) - T(x_{i}, t_{i})] + [T(x_{i} - dx, t_{i}) - T(x_{i}, t_{i})] \)
